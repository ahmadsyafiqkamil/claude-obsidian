# Source: Digital Identity Patterns

**Type**: Academic / overview reference
**Confidence**: high

## Key Claims

- Digital identity = personal data enabling automated access to digital services and authentication online
- Core technical functions: issuance, trust/authentication/authorization, verification, digital identifiers, risk-based authentication
- National digital ID systems (Estonia e-Residency, Singapore Singpass, India Aadhaar) underpin e-government and consular services
- Identity wallets and verifiable credentials are emerging patterns for cross-border document use

## Significance for Consular Service Management

[[Tanda Tangan Elektronik]] (TTE) is Indonesia's implementation of the "trust/authentication/authorization" function for digital identity. PROTKONS uses TTE credentials (`tte_nik`, `tte_pwd`) for Pejabat Penanda Tangan (HS) signing of consular documents.

The emerging pattern of **identity wallets + verifiable credentials** (W3C VC standard) is a future trajectory that could replace per-document TTE stamping with portable credentials that the WNI presents at any government counter.
