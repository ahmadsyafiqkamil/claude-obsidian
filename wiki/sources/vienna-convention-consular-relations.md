# Source: Vienna Convention on Consular Relations (1963)

**Type**: International treaty / Wikipedia summary
**Confidence**: high (international law, multiple ratifications)

## Key Claims

- Article 5 of the Vienna Convention on Consular Relations (1963) is the internationally accepted definition of consular functions (Source: [[Consular Service Management]])
- Standard consular functions: replacement travel documents, support during illness/death/crime, prison visitation, crisis help, overseas social welfare payments, citizen birth registration abroad, local doctor/lawyer referrals, flag vessel supervision
- Explicit exclusions: luggage storage, commercial disputes, banking/postal, money exchange, translation, legal advice, bail negotiation, criminal investigation, employment services

## Relevance to PROTKONS / Chatbot KJRI Dubai

The standard service catalogue in Article 5 matches the PROTKONS workflow (Draft → Pengajuan → Verifikasi → Penandatanganan → Pembayaran → Penyerahan → Arsip). The exclusions list informs what the Chatbot KJRI Dubai must NOT answer (no legal advice, no commercial dispute mediation, no criminal investigation guidance).
