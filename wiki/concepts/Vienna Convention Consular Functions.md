# Vienna Convention Consular Functions

Article 5 of the **Vienna Convention on Consular Relations (1963)** is the authoritative international framework that defines what consular services are. Every Indonesian KBRI/KJRI is bound by these functions (Indonesia ratified 1982).

## Standard Service Catalogue

- Replacement travel documents (paspor sementara)
- Citizen protection during illness, death, crime abroad
- Prison visitation
- Crisis assistance (disaster, conflict, evacuation)
- Overseas social welfare payments
- Citizen birth registration abroad
- Local doctor/lawyer referrals
- Flag vessel supervision (kapal berbendera Indonesia)

## Explicit Exclusions

Consular officers must NOT provide:
- Luggage storage
- Commercial dispute resolution
- Banking/postal services
- Money exchange
- Translation (general)
- Legal advice
- Bail negotiation
- Criminal investigation
- Employment services
