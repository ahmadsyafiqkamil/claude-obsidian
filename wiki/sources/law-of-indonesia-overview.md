# Source: Law of Indonesia (overview)

**Type**: Wikipedia overview of Indonesian legal system
**Confidence**: medium (Wikipedia summary; for specific consular laws, primary Permenlu/Permenkumham docs would be higher)

## Key Claims

- Indonesian law is based on Roman-Dutch civil law with some customary (adat) and Islamic (sharia) influences
- Hierarchy: Constitution (UUD 1945) → Laws (UU) → Government Regulations (PP) → Presidential Regulations (Perpres) → Ministerial Regulations (Permen) → Local Regulations
- Key consular-relevant statutes (not verified directly in this fetch — flagged for verification):
  - UU No. 1 Tahun 1982 — tentang Ketentuan Umum Konsuler (general consular provisions)
  - UU No. 11 Tahun 2008 jo UU No. 19 Tahun 2016 — Informasi dan Transaksi Elektronik (ITE) — legal basis for TTE
  - PP No. 71 Tahun 2019 — Penyelenggaraan Sistem Elektronik
  - Permenlu various — consular service procedures
